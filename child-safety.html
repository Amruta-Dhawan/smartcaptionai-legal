Child Safety Standards (CSAE)

SmartCaptionAI - Virajo AutoSoft Pvt. Ltd.

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Our Commitment to Child Safety

SmartCaptionAI is committed to protecting children who may use our application. We comply with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, including child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) prevention requirements.

Age Restrictions

SmartCaptionAI is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Content Moderation

  • User-generated content (videos) is stored locally or in user's private cloud storage
  • No public content sharing or social features
  • No user-to-user communication features
  • No comments, messaging, or community features

Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) Prevention

We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM). Our practices include:

  • No public uploads: All videos remain private to the user
  • No sharing platform: App does not host or distribute content publicly
  • Immediate action: Any suspected CSAM will be reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and law enforcement
  • User education: Terms of service prohibit illegal content

Privacy Protection for Minors

  • No collection of personal information from users under 13
  • Parental consent required for users under 13 (if applicable)
  • Minimal data collection - only email for account creation
  • No location tracking of minors
  • No behavioral advertising targeting children

Reporting Mechanism

If you become aware of any child safety concerns related to SmartCaptionAI:

Report to:
Email: appsupport@virajo.in
Subject: "Child Safety Concern - URGENT"

We will respond within 24 hours and take immediate action.

Compliance with Laws

SmartCaptionAI complies with:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) - United States
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 - India
  • Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012 - India

Data Handling for Minors

For users under 18:

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • No third-party access to user videos (except AssemblyAI for caption processing only)
  • Users can delete their account and data at any time
  • No data sold or shared with advertisers

Staff Training

All team members with access to user data receive training on:

  • Child safety principles
  • CSAM recognition and reporting
  • Privacy protection for minors
  • Compliance with child protection laws

Virajo AutoSoft Pvt. Ltd.
