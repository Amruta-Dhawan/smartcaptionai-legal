Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go! If you wish to delete your SmartCaptionAI account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

What Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password
  • Video Library: All saved videos and captions
  • Usage Data: Processing history and statistics
  • Preferences: App settings and customizations

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from App (Recommended)

  1. Open the SmartCaptionAI app
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Scroll down to Account Settings
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted
  6. Your account will be deleted immediately

Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer email, send a deletion request to:

📧 Send Deletion Request

Email: appsupport@virajo.in

Please include in your email:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request - SmartCaptionAI"
  • Your registered email address
  • Your account name
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
⏱️ Processing Time: In-app deletions are immediate. Email requests are processed within 7 business days.

Data Retention After Deletion

After account deletion:

  • Immediate: Account access is revoked
  • 7 Days: Personal data removed from active systems
  • 30 Days: All data permanently deleted from backups
  • Legal Retention: Anonymized data may be retained for legal compliance

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives:

  • Take a Break: Log out without deleting
  • Export Your Data: Download your videos before deletion
  • Contact Support: We can help resolve any issues

Need Help?

Questions or concerns? Contact us:

  • Email: appsupport@virajo.in
