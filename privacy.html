Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Virajo AutoSoft Pvt. Ltd. ("we," "us," or "our") operates SmartCaptionAI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Video Content

  • Videos you upload for caption generation are processed temporarily
  • Videos are not permanently stored on our servers unless you save them to your library
  • Generated captions are saved to your account

Usage Data

  • App interactions and features used
  • Number of videos processed
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered caption generation services
  • Store your video library and preferences
  • Improve our AI models and app features
  • Communicate with you about updates and support
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/SSL
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt
  • Account data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas
  • Videos are processed on secure servers
  • We implement regular security updates and monitoring

4. Third-Party Services

AssemblyAI

We use AssemblyAI for speech-to-text processing. Your videos are processed according to their privacy policy. AssemblyAI does not retain your audio data after processing. Learn more at: AssemblyAI Privacy Policy

Infrastructure Providers

  • Railway: Backend hosting
  • MongoDB Atlas: Database storage
  • Google Play Services: App distribution

5. Data Retention

  • Uploaded videos: Deleted immediately after caption generation (unless saved to library)
  • Library videos: Retained until you delete them or your account
  • Account information: Retained until you delete your account
  • Usage data: Retained for up to 2 years for analytics

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@smartcaptionai.com

7. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With service providers (AssemblyAI, hosting providers) necessary to operate the app
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent

8. Children's Privacy

SmartCaptionAI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using SmartCaptionAI, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

10. Cookies and Tracking

SmartCaptionAI does not use cookies. We use local storage on your device to save your preferences and login session. This data never leaves your device unless explicitly synced.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification
  • Showing an in-app notification

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
